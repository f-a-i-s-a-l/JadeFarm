It is with an extremely heavy heart that JUPC informs all that an act of vandalism was observed today when we found one of our exhibition setups near the Central Libray torn apart. It was quite easy to conclude that it was torn by humans and not due to natural phenomena like wind or rain. This year we conducted the exhibitions in the campus in order to give more people from the University an opportunity to engage with JUPC and various photographic practices as well. But these kind of unfortunate incidents make it terribly hard to keep alive our will to do good events in the campus in future. In the absence of any concrete proof we are unable to make any accusations against anyone but this is a request to the administration to look into these matter(s) so that such incidents could be prevented in future. #montage20 #jupc #vandalism #jadavpuruniversity
